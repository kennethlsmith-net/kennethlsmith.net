Program Information

The radio show starred Lucille Ball and Richard Denning as Liz and George Cugat (later episodes change the last name to Cooper). The couple lived at 321 Bundy Drive in the fictitious city of Sheridan Falls, and were billed as "two people who live together and like it." The program initially portrayed the couple as being a well-to-do banker and his socially prominent wife.

The first episode of My Favorite Husband aired July 23, 1948. Also, an earlier "audition" episode aired July 5,1948. The last of 126 episodes aired March 13,1951. CBS Radio Network aired the programs.

It aired on the CBS Radio network on Friday nights from July 23, 1948, to September 24, 1948, on Saturday nights from October 2, 1948, to December 25, 1948, on Friday nights from January 7, 1949, to February 24, 1950, on Sunday nights from March 5, 1950, to June 25, 1950, and on Saturday nights from September 2, 1950, to March 31, 1951.
